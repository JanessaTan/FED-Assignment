Terms & Conditions

Key Terms and Conditions for Restaurant Lease Agreements:

  • Approval & Usage: Tenants must obtain all necessary approvals before starting business operations, and adhere to permitted trades.
  • Space & Setup: For HDB premises, only up to 50% of the area can be sub-let, and specific, often full-height, walled-up, central kitchen, and non-partitioned setups are required for food preparation.
  • Infrastructure: Proper installation of exhaust systems and grease traps is mandatory, with all costs borne by the tenant.
  • Operating Restrictions: Common areas cannot be used as outdoor refreshment areas, and there may be limitations on operating hours or changes to the trade.
  • Financial & Legal: Tenants must pay base rent plus additional charges like Common Area Maintenance (CAM), utilities, and insurance.
  • Tenant Obligation: Adherence to safety regulations (fire safety, exit signs) is mandatory.
